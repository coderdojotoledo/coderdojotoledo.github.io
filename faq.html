Frequently Asked Questions

"How do I sign up?"

Go to the Register page on our website and sign up for our newsletter. You will receive more information when you get our newsletter.

"How old does my child have to be?"

We accept kids ranging from age 5 to age 17.

"What does my child need to bring to each session?"

A laptop (Including Chromebook) and if your child is under 13, you must stay with them!

"When do the sessions take place and where?"

The sessions are every Saturday from 12:00 - 3:00. Each session is hosted at Potter Technologies at 9750 Clark Road, Rossford, Ohio.

"How can we contact the supervisors?"

You can contact Scott at scott.thompson@coderdojotoledo.com or Jack at jakku.thompson@coderdojotoledo.com.
